You're not alone

1 in 5 teens currently struggle with a mental health disorder

Statistics

Those who struggle with mental health are:

-3x more likely to be disengaged with school (43.9% vs. 14.9%)

-5x more likely to miss 11 or more days due to health reasons (17.7% vs. 3.5%)

-2x more likely to be a victim of bullying (60.5% vs. 27.2%)

-10x more likely to have a hard time making or keeping friends (20.4% vs. 2.1%)
