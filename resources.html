Resources

Here are some helpful resources for you, while you're on your mental health journey.

Support groups

The Asian mental health collective is a group that is dedicated to providing support for those struggling with mental health both in person and online. They offer online support groups for those who need it.

Community Support Groups – Asian Mental Health Collective

The Asian mental health collective also offer individual therapy for those who need it. They have several free programs that you can apply for like the lotus therapy fund (LTF), which offers 8 free virtual sessions or 3 months of in-person sessions for free.

Free Therapy Funds – Asian Mental Health Collective

National Hotlines

Suicide and crisis lines

Call or text 988

Webchat at https://chat.988lifeline.org/ There are options on this website for spanish and those who are hard of hearing

The Trevor Project Suicide Lifeline

The Trevor Project can help anyone but they were made specifically for the LGBTQ+ community.

Call 1(866)488-7386 or Text “START” to 678-678

Webchat at chat.trvr.org or vist their homepage

Crisis text line

Text “HELLO” to 741741 for English or Text “HOLA” to 741741 for Spanish

Web chat at connect.crisistextline.org/chat or visit their website

National Domestic Violence Hotline

Call (800)799-7233 or Text “START” to 88788 to get help and their services are available in both Spanish and English.

Webchat at www.thehotline.org

The Deaf Hotline

Hotline with an asl option https://www.thedeafhotline.org/

Resource for the deaf and mentally ill in DC https://deafdawn.org/
